About

First Bell MMA is a media company covering professional mixed martial arts at the regional level. We produce interviews, event coverage, and original content focused on the emerging talent pipeline across the sport.

The mainstream covers fighters after they arrive. We cover them on the way up — the regional cards, the local circuits, the gyms and camps where careers are built before anyone's watching. Our interviews capture fighters at the inflection point between unknown and undeniable.

We attend events, build relationships with promotions, and create content that documents the sport at a level most outlets ignore. When a fighter we've covered signs with a major promotion, we were there first. That's the point.

First Bell MMA publishes weekly interviews and event content across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and X.
