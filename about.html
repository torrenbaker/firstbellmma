About

First Bell MMA is a media company covering professional mixed martial arts across the professional landscape.

We produce interviews, event coverage, and original content focused on the emerging talent pipeline across the sport. The mainstream covers fighters after they arrive. We cover them on the way up.

We attend events, build relationships with promotions, and document the sport at a level most outlets ignore. When a fighter we've covered signs with a major promotion, we were already there.

First Bell MMA publishes weekly content across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and X.
